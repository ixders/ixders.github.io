Top 5 reasons why I get into interactive media

Posted on 2016-09-21   |   In assignment , immt718

Over the last twenty years, the internet has now deeply entrenched in our lives and influences almost everything. The traditional media such as newspaper, magazines and other publications are beginning to disappear as digital media gets stronger than ever. As a professional web and user interface designer who have been structuring and designing websites since the “early days” in 2000, I experienced astonishing changes and innovations in technology. After 16 years, I’d like to talk about what intrigued me a career in interactive media.

1) As a typical enthusiast of the internet, I am passionate about cutting-edge technologies in virtual reality, augmented reality, wearable devices, artificial intelligence, 360 panorama video and so on. The emerging trends in digital media fascinate me all the time.


Chinese wearable device: Mi Band 2


2) I really appreciate working closely with some of my former colleagues who are highly creative people in this industry (Art Director, Product Designer, Graphic Designer, Web Developer, Project Manager, Marketing Manager, Online Editor, etc…) and I regard them as my teacher in the real world.


After 16 years, I had a party with my former colleagues and boss. After graduation in 2000, it was my first job for an online advertising company in Shanghai.


3) From a designer’s perspective, I love the diversity of this industry. I prefer to be a freelancer (project based contract) for different kinds of media and most importantly - there is flexibility on how I get to manage my own time and work.


I used to work overtime in office.


4) China’s digital media market is a miracle in the world, a story of an internet supergiant. There is an increasing trend in entrepreneurship in the first-tier cities in China. The majority of venture capital firms never stop looking for young entrepreneurs. I have seen numerous new graduate students running their start-ups with entrepreneurial potential in this industry.


The modern sculpture represents the entrepreneurship of young generation in Shanghai.


5) My 3-year-old daughter was really interested in playing iPad games although sometimes she was tapping screen without purpose. Today’s kids confidently roam rich virtual worlds. As a mother, I have strong passion about learning digital products for kids ages 1 to 5.
