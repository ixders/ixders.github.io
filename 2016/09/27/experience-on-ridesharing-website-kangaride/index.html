Sign up for student account or not? Experience on ridesharing website – Kangaride.com

Posted on 2016-09-27   |   In user experience
Introduction

Kangaride.com is a ridesharing platform in Canada and USA. It puts drivers and passengers in contact for long-distance rides so they can share the road and the costs of the trip.

Objective

Identify pain points in Kangaride’s current registration, searching and booking process.

Scenario

I am a post-secondary student who is currently living in Scarborough, Toronto. This weekend I recalled that I had promised my friend Ting in Montreal to help her moving into their newly purchased house next month. My friend told me the pre-scheduled moving date is October 2, Sunday afternoon, which happens next week and now I need to buy a bus ticket from Toronto to Montreal. In order to save money on trip, I decided to reserve a seat through one of the ridesharing websites in Canada – Kangaride. I’m excited to get important information through the website: Kangaride provides six months’ free trial membership for students.

Tasks

Book a seat in a posted ride by using free trial membership account for student and most importantly – it should match my ride criteria.

1) To create a free trial membership for students as I am a passenger.

2) To log in my student account.

3) To search for a scheduled ride for a specific date range. The search results must correspond to my ride requirements.

4) To book a seat based on ride searching results.

Implementation process

I need to create a free trial membership for students.

Step 1: I click the yellow button of create account which is located at the top right side of the homepage, it links to the page “Create a Profile”.

Step 2: At the right section of page, I click the text “Get your free trial membership”, it links to the page of “Free membership for students”.

Step 3: I select education institution from the list, “Centennial College” is successfully founded in the list, I click the item and it links to a form page which has instruction about validation of student status.

Step 4: I fill in the web form including my full name and email address of my school. Click the button of “Submit this form”. Then, there is an error message in red appears on the page: “This email does not seem to be associated with an educational institution, college or university. However, if this email address is your student email, please contact us at: 1-855-526-4274 (toll-free)”

Step5: In terms of specific instruction which indicates: “Send us an email at info@kangaride.com with proof of your student status, like a copy of your schedule or student card.” Alternatively, I choose the way by sending email to Kangaride.

Results

Until September 27, in order to get validation of my student status, I have been waiting for the confirmation email from Kangaride for several days. I am not able to accomplish the task: Book a seat in a posted ride by using free trial membership account for student. So I decide to leave Kangaride and to go through rideshare posts on Kijiji.

Key findings

As a college student, I am not sure whether I am going to get a free trial membership or not as there is no terms of condition or clue defines “Who can apply for a student membership?” I’m wondering whether it is for post-secondary students or high school students or mature students. I believe this issue might confuse users.

The lists of educational institution displayed on web page is too long and it is mix up with Canadian post-secondary schools and American’s. The web page might be slow to load because of a bunch of school lists.

I got frustrated as I saw so many annoying and repeated messages while going through lists of education institution. “Kangaride is coming to your college soon! Be the first to get a free trial account*!” Actually I was scared of clicking that text link. What exactly would happen afterwards?”
