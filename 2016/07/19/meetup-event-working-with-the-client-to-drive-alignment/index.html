Meetup Event - Working with the client to drive alignment

Posted on 2016-07-19   |   In meetup

It’s exciting for me to join group Agile XD Toronto as agile product management plays an important part through working process in business.

That’s two great presentations on site. What I mentioned is several students from Sheridan College just sitting beside me. As a professor, Andrew’s pedagogy for this lecture is heuristic by asking questions and to use a typical gift box in white as physical prop.

Thanks for Rangle’s efforts!! At last, I found two lecture videos via their company website.

Summary

Join us to hear and learn from our featured speakers Andrew Smyk, Professor and Program Co-ordinator at Sheridan’s Interactive Media Management Post-Graduate Program and Naomi Bower, Product Designer at Rangle.io

Session #1 - From Digital to Physical and Back Again

Andrew will demonstrate how your development/design team can brainstorm and contextualize features and presentation layers by thinking of digital projects as physical products. He will also discuss how to promote primary features and user messaging by applying this approach.

Session #2 - Stakeholder Experience Design

Rangle’s very own Product Designer Naomi Bower discusses how your project’s success depends on the first experience you design for stakeholders. Using key design principles, Naomi will demonstrate how teams can create empathy and achieve business goals by building enjoyable and action-oriented experiences.

Topics Covered:
  • Why it’s important to build empathy for stakeholders
  • How making stakeholders feel comfortable and in control actually helps your team
  • How to facilitate a better project kickoff by applying a lightweight and repeatable structure
  • An overview Rangle’s kickoff and discovery process
